  • Wood: ~$82/ft avg (868 projects, avg total $4,468)
  • Vinyl: ~$71/ft avg (10 projects, avg total $8,434)
  • Chain Link: ~$41/ft avg (25 projects, avg total $4,189)
  • Aluminum: ~$47/ft avg

The average Dallas fence project is about 89 linear feet. Final price varies with height, gates, terrain, and project scope.

Based on recent projects in Dallas, the average time from winning the project to completion is about ~5 weeks (including scheduling, permits where needed, and installation). On-site installation itself takes about 60 linear feet per day in clear conditions — so a 100-ft run is ~1-2 days of active work.
In Dallas, residential fence height is set by zoning and yard location. Most lots allow: side yards up to ~6 ft, rear yards up to 6 ft, front yards typically lower for sightlines. Decorative elements (lattice, arched caps) may increase total height where permitted. Corner lots have additional sightline restrictions.
